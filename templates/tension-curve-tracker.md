## Warning Rules

- **Sag:** ≥ 2 chapters in a row with **T ≤ 3** → pacing finding (structure finding `SF`), not just a local revision.
- **Clarity-debt trend:** C on average < T over an act window → address clarity systemically (not chapter by chapter).
- **Climax expectation:** end of an act without a T peak → check structure before the next act begins.

## Usage

- Reader delivers T/C → the conductor enters them here **and** in the synopsis register.
- At the act transition: check the curve against the target before writing on.
