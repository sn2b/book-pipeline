> **Purpose:** Prevent the classic info leak — a character acts on knowledge they cannot have yet, or a twist becomes visible too early. ## Check Rules (FactChecker)

1. **Action without knowledge:** Does a character make a decision that presupposes knowledge that, per the table, is not yet "since Ch?"? → **Blocker** (info leak).
2. **Premature reveal:** Does a revelation become visible before its reveal-schedule chapter is reached? → **Finding**, have the author confirm.
3. **Deliberate dramatic irony:** If the reader knows more than a character, it must be intended that way in "to whom first" — otherwise a suspected slip.

## Maintenance

The Worldbuilder updates the table per chapter (new "since Ch?" entries). FactChecker checks every chapter against it.
