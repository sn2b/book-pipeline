# Setup→Payoff Register (Template)

> **Purpose:** Within **one** book, every planted seed (Chekhov's gun, hint, foreshadowing) must either be paid off or deliberately marked as a sequel seed. Without a register, threads dangle, or a payoff lands unprepared. Lives at `canon/setup-payoff-register.md`.

## Status Legend

- 🌱 **planted** — setup is in place, payoff still open (expected).
- ✅ **paid off** — payoff in the text, the reference holds.
- 🔁 **sequel** — deliberately not resolved in this book (seed for a continuation).
- ⚠️ **orphaned** — planted but with no planned payoff → **finding**: pay off or remove.

## Control Points

- **Per chapter:** enter new seeds (🌱), set paid-off ones to ✅ — in sync with the synopsis register.
- **Before the final act:** check every 🌱. Any that is not ✅ or deliberately 🔁 is ⚠️ and must be decided.
- **Before the book closes:** no ⚠️ open. Any remaining 🔁 explicitly documented as a sequel seed.

## Owner

The Worldbuilder maintains the register (seeds touch canon/plot); the conductor checks it at act transitions and before the finale.
