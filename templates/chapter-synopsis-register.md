# Chapter Synopsis Register (Template)

> **Purpose:** A one-paragraph synopsis per chapter as the **first reading source** for all agents. Saves context (no full reread/grep across all chapters), and contradictions become visible here **before** they get written into the text. Lives at `canon/chapter-synopsis-register.md`.
>
> **Maintenance:** The Worldbuilder (or conductor) enters the synopsis after each completed chapter. Writer/Editor/FactChecker read this register **before** accessing the actual chapter.

## Usage

1. **Before each run:** the conductor passes the relevant synopses (previous/following chapters) into the brief instead of the full texts.
2. **Contradiction found:** synopsis does not match the planned chapter → cross-consistency stop, author/Worldbuilder clarify.
3. **After completion:** enter the new synopsis; sync planted/paid-off seeds with the setup-payoff-register.
